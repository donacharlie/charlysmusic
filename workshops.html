Workshops

  • Sound Awakening Journey

    In this children workshop, we focuse in awakening to sound and music, in awareness of what is surrounding us, and in relaxation.
    Through playful and educational games, instruments, sounds, songs, dances, adventures, discoveries and relaxation, we will explore a world of Music.
    Music and fun activities will help children in their learning process and well-being but also in developping respect and listening skills (of someone else but also of oneself), awareness of the body and also our feelings, autonomy, creativity, imagination, and self confidence.
    For more infos about this workshop contact Charlene at donacharlie@yahoo.fr

  • Music For Fun

    Playing music for fun!
    In this group we will explore rhythms, singing, different instruments and we will lear to play together as a band or ensemble in a playful way. We will develop musical and artistic skills, listening skills, self-esteem and confidence, and maybe a little show? ;)
    For more info contact Charlene at donacharlie@yahoo.fr

  • Music Lessons

    Violin lessons: classical, folk, traditionnal, world music, improvisation.
    Guitar for beginner, folk music, chords, accompaniement.
    Music Theory for begginner and intermediate.
    Private or small groups

  • Meditative Sound Journey

    In this workshop we will explore Music, well being, relaxation, awareness, observation and letting go of our feelings through sounds,instruments, storytelling, meditation...
    Some influences for this workshop are soundbathing and yoga nidra.
    This activity aims at rediscovering music while developping well being, imagination, self-esteem and confidence or simply have some peaceful fun ;)
    For more infos contact Charlene at donacharlie@yahoo.fr
